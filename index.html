What is Roblox VNG

On Easyfun, you'll find different Roblox instances like Doors, Pet Simulator 99, Adopt Me, and Brookhaven RP that give you access to the global version of Roblox. However, you will also find an instance called VNG, which leads to the Vietnam-only version of Roblox.

Please be careful: Never try to play the VNG version unless you are already familiar with it. This version is controlled and monitored by the Vietnamese government. While it lets you join global players, some global features might not be accessible.

Most importantly: Once you log into the VNG version of Roblox, you cannot switch back to the global version. Your account will be locked to VNG permanently. Make sure to choose the correct game instance!

Custom Key Mapping for Easy Play

Playing mobile games on PC can be tricky, especially when you're stuck using just your mouse for controls. But with Easyfun, that’s not even an issue. You can play games using both your mouse and keyboard seamlessly.

Roblox, for example, has tons of games where the controls you like in one game might not work for another. That’s where Easyfun’s custom key mapping comes to the rescue! You can set up keys to match your play style and switch things up whenever you need. It’s quick to set up, and once saved, your settings will be ready for the next time you play.

Custom key mapping isn’t just about assigning keys to activate skills. You can take your gameplay to the next level with advanced setups like one-button combos. For example, in Blox fruit, you can map a single key to handle actions like activating a fruit ability, using a sword attack, or switching fighting styles.

Here’s a trick:

  1. Set one key for double clicks and assign it to two actions—like switching to a fruit skill and activating the first ability.

  2. While using your fighting style, press this key twice to fire off the skill.

  3. Quickly switch back to your fighting style by pressing the dedicated key.

Want to go even further? Add another click, assign the fighting style key to it, map it to fighting skill, this way you can fire attack skill while swapping between fighting style and firing of fruit abilities. If you repeat this setup for all your skills. Now you’ve got a faster, smoother skill-firing method that’ll leave your opponents stunned.
